# Hypothesis

Claim:
Many students can provide a correct definition of a concept but cannot explain why it exists, what problem it solves, or what would happen without it.

Success Criteria:
At least 60% of tested users reveal a meaningful gap in understanding when asked targeted follow-up derivation questions.

Evidence:
The application successfully identifies the knowledge gap and provides feedback that helps the user improve their explanation.

No Problem Exists If:
Fewer than 30% of tested users reveal a meaningful gap in understanding after the initial explanation.

Kill Number:
If fewer than 20% of tested users reveal a meaningful gap in understanding, this hypothesis is considered false.
